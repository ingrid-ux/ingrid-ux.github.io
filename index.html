ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE

We were found in 2016 by two dessert enthusiasts whose love for ice cream led to creating SomiSomi. 4 years later, Somisomi can be found all over the U.S. today, offering irresistible Korean-inspired soft serve in ornate goldfish-shaped waffles cones.​

Our company delivers personalized ice cream experience with a Korean flare that is truly unique and unforgettable.
